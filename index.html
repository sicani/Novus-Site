Welcome to advanced living.

How we operate

Ease into Green Life

One of our main goals at Novus is to help people become better acquainted with renewable energy. We provide solar, hydro, and wind powered energy sources.

Maintain Comfort

While adjusting to new energy is important to us, it's also vital to show users that we can still enjoy the luxuries of a modern lifestyle.

Track Your Progress

Each living space comes equipped with our own personal software that accurately tracks your use of daily utilities such as electricity and water. Our software is designed to help users gradually decrease their use of said resources.

Enjoy Down Time

We want customers spending time in our community to relax and enjoy the luxurious format of our living spaces. While we want you to take away how effective renewable energy can be, we also want the entire experience to be a pleasant time.

Company Goals

Mission Statement

Build a unique and high-end living experience centered on reducing human impact on the environment.

Vision Statement

Utilize cutting-edge design and innovative technology to create a community based on solar power. Introduce society to the benefits of efficient and renewable energy sources.

Start Small

It’s important for societies to recognize that our day-to-day resources will not be around forever. Not only are these resources depleting, but most of them produce harmful emissions which damage the environment.

We hope that once people experience our community, it will motivate them - even if just a little bit. If someone decides to install solar panels on their roof after visiting our community then that’s a win for us. While we are a small company, every person working with us will help make a difference.
